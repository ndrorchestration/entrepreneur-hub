## Why Governance-First

AI products fail audits, stall enterprise adoption, and accumulate invisible risk — not because they lack features, but because they lack a governance posture.

This hub packages the [DGAF Framework](https://github.com/ndrorchestration/DGAF-Framework) (33+ active patterns, NIST AI RMF + EU AI Act aligned) into consumable Needle templates that:

- Cut pre-launch governance review from **4–6 hours → automated**
- Produce audit-ready artifacts out of the box
- Scale from solo builder to enterprise team without re-architecture
